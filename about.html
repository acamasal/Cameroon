About ACAMASAL

ACAMASAL is a vibrant and united community of Cameroonians living in Austria. Our association aims to:

  • Promote unity and cultural pride among Cameroonians in Austria
  • Support new arrivals with integration and resources
  • Organize events that celebrate our heritage and foster collaboration
  • Build bridges with other African and Austrian communities
